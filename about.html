About Friendillion

I'm Full Stack Developer, Web Content Writer, and a Serial Entrepreneur.
I created Friendillion specifically to change the world one Friendship at a time. If most of the people from every parts of the world embrace this belief, our world will definitely be a better place to live in.

Collaboration is the key. The more that we help each other, the more projects, more businesses, and more success to our goals.
We will also gain new friends along the way, may it be an Angel Investor, a Business Partner, or a fellow Web Developer who can help with us in our project.
The more new friends, the more new good things that we will come our way.

Here's to our success!
