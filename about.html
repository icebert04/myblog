About Friendillion

I'm a Web Developer, Digital Content Writer, and a Serial Entrepreneur.

I created "Friendillion" to gather interesting people in the Web Development Realm.
That "New Friend" could be an Angel Investor, a Business Partner, or a fellow Web Developer who can help both you and me in our project(s).

My goal is to build that list and show their inspiring stories to you.
You can get some ideas, insights, and even work from these individuals. They too could be your "New Friend" as well.

We are not alone in our journey.
There are people out there who could help us. Collaboration is the key.
The more that we help each other, the more projects, more businesses, and more success that will come our way.

Let's collect Friends until we reach Millions (Friend-illion).
It's good to be a Millionaire with Money, but it's better to be a Millionaire having quality Friends.

Here's to our success!
